Common questions
I've tried so many things before and nothing has worked. How is this different?
Most approaches treat the symptoms: a new diet, a supplement, a pill. This is about understanding the root cause of what's driving your symptoms, so that everything else you do actually makes sense and sticks. You're not starting another plan. You're finally getting the education your body has needed.
I don't know where to start. Is this suitable for a complete beginner?
Yes, completely. The academy is built from the ground up, starting with the fundamentals of your cycle and hormones before anything else. If you've ever felt overwhelmed by conflicting information online, this gives you one clear, structured place to start.
I have PCOS / endometriosis / fibroids. Is this relevant for me?
Absolutely. Hormone-related conditions like PCOS, endometriosis, adenomyosis and fibroids are covered directly in the curriculum. You'll understand how these conditions develop, what's driving them in your body, and what you can actually do to support yourself.
My doctor says everything looks normal but I still feel terrible. Can this help?
This is one of the most common experiences in Jenni's community. Standard blood tests don't always capture the full picture of hormonal health. This academy fills the gap between "your results are normal" and actually feeling well. Your symptoms are real, even when labs don't show it.
I'm really busy. How much time does this require?
The modules are pre-recorded so you learn completely at your own pace, with no pressure to keep up. The live weekly Q&A is the only scheduled element, and even that is there to support you, not stress you out. This is built for real life.
